Hi, I want to tell you about my mornings. I wake up at six thirty every day. My alarm clock is loud, so I can't sleep in. First, I open the window. The fresh air feels really nice. Then I drink a big glass of water.

After that, I go to the kitchen. I make some coffee and toast. I like my toast with butter and jam. My cat wants food too, so I feed her first. She's always hungry in the morning. While I eat, I check the weather. Today it's sunny, so I don't need my umbrella.

Next, I take a quick shower. I brush my teeth and get dressed. I don't have much time, so I move fast. Sometimes I can't find my keys. That's the worst part of my morning. I always look for them at the last minute.

I leave my house at seven forty. The train station is close to me. It's about a ten minute walk. I like walking because I see the same people every day. We don't talk, but we smile. It's a nice, quiet start to the day.

My morning isn't very exciting, but I like it. A calm morning makes me feel ready. When I skip breakfast, my whole day feels wrong. So I try to keep my routine simple. What's your morning like? I hope it's a good one.
