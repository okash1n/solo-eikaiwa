I usually do my laundry on Sunday morning. It's a small habit, but I kind of like it. This week, though, the weather didn't help me at all. I woke up, opened the curtains, and saw grey clouds. It was going to rain, and my basket was already full.

I thought about waiting for a sunny day. But I was almost out of clean shirts for work. So I decided to just do it anyway. I put everything in the machine, added the soap, and pressed the button. Then I made a coffee and waited for the beep.

Here's my problem. I live in a small flat, and I don't have a dryer. On a sunny day, I hang everything on the balcony. But on a rainy day like this, that's no good. Wet clothes plus rain? That doesn't work.

So I hung the clothes inside instead. I put a rack near the window and opened it a little for air. I also turned on a fan to help them dry faster. It's not perfect. The room feels a bit humid, and things take longer to dry.

By the evening, most of my shirts were dry enough. Not all of them, but enough for Monday. I folded them while watching TV. It wasn't a fun job, but I felt good after. Sometimes the boring little tasks make the week feel calmer.
