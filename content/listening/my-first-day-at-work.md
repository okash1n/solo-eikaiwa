Hi, my name's Kenji. Last week I started a new job. I work at a small office in the city. I was really nervous on my first day. I got up early and put on my new clothes. I didn't want to be late.

I got to the office at nine. A woman named Mrs. Tanaka met me at the door. She's my new boss, and she's very kind. She showed me my desk and my computer. Then she gave me a cup of coffee. That helped me feel calm.

In the morning, I met my team. There are five people in my group. They all said hello and smiled at me. One man showed me how to use the phone. Another woman told me about our work. I tried hard to remember all their names.

At lunch, we went to a small shop near the office. I ate rice and fish with my new friends. We talked about food and hobbies. It was fun, and I wasn't nervous anymore. I'm happy I can eat lunch with nice people.

In the afternoon, I read some papers and sent a few emails. The work isn't easy, but I like it. My boss said I did a good job. I felt so proud when I heard that. I can't wait to go back tomorrow.
