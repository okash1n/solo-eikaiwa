title_ja: "会議前のスモールトーク"

Talk about:
- How your week has been going — 今週の調子
- Weather or seasons where you live — 住んでいる場所の天気・季節の話
- Something nice that happened recently — 最近あったちょっと良いこと
- A question you can ask the other person back — 相手に返す質問の例
